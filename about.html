About Us

Sumi's Beauty is a private, women-only hair care service designed especially for hijabi and niqabi sisters who prefer a comfortable, modest environment. We bring professional, all-natural hair care right to your home — no extensions, no exposure, just authentic beauty and self-care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make every woman feel confident and cared for while respecting her privacy and values. Whether you're looking for a simple trim, natural treatment, or a fresh style, we offer affordable, high-quality home services throughout Colchester and surrounding areas.
